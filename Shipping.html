Shipping Information

Delivery Areas

We deliver across India. All major cities receive standard and express shipping options. Same-day delivery is currently available in Mumbai and Delhi metropolitan areas.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed before 2 PM on weekdays are typically shipped the same day. Weekend orders are processed on Monday.

Packaging

All products are carefully packed in protective packaging to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Temperature-sensitive supplements are shipped with appropriate cooling materials when necessary.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your package in real-time using our tracking system or the courier's website.

Delivery Schedule

Deliveries are made Monday through Saturday, 9 AM to 7 PM. Sunday delivery is available for express and same-day shipping in select areas. Someone must be available to receive the package.

Shipping Restrictions

  • PO Box addresses are not accepted
  • Some remote areas may have additional delivery time
  • Certain products may have shipping restrictions
  • International shipping is not currently available

Shipping Support

If you have any questions about shipping or need to modify your delivery address, contact our customer service team at +91 84518 83938 or email us before your order ships.
